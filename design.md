# Design System & UI/UX Guidelines

## 1. Core Aesthetic
The overarching theme is a transition from a **mysterious, high-tech vault** to a **warm, romantic, and dreamy floral universe**. The UI heavily relies on **Glassmorphism** to ensure text remains readable while the animated floral backgrounds shine through.

## 2. Color Palette
- **The Vault (Lock Screen):** Deep Obsidian (`#0F0F13`), Neon Pink Accents (`#FF2A55`).
- **Scene 1 & 2 (Dawn & The Bouquet):** Soft Pink & Crimson Gradients (`#2B1B3D` to `#E58B9C`), complementing the vibrant reds and pinks of the virtual flowers.
- **Scene 3 & 4 (Sunset / Memories):** Warm Oranges & Deep Reds (`#FF7B54`, `#C1292E`).
- **Scene 5 (Midnight / Finale):** Deep Starry Blue/Black (`#0B0C10`, `#1F2833`).

## 3. Typography
- **Headings & Special Text:** `Playfair Display`. Elegant, serif, and highly romantic for titles.
- **Body & Letters:** `Outfit` or `Inter`. Clean, modern sans-serif for high readability inside the glass cards.

## 4. UI Components & The Floral Theme
- **The Virtual Bouquet:** High-quality PNG/WebP images of beautiful flowers (roses, peonies, etc.) arranged dynamically at the top of the page.
- **Glass Cards (for 3D interactions):** 
  - `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05);`
  - `backdrop-filter: blur(15px);`
- **The Parallax Petals:** Softly blurred petal images layered in the background to give a constant sense of romance and movement.
- **The Beating Heart Button:** A glowing radial gradient with a soft, pulsing drop shadow.
